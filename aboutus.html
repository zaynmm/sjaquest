About Us

Our mission

We are high school students taking part in the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program. Our goal for this page is to not only raise awareness of the silenced topic of sexual assaults on college campuses and otherwise, but also create a safe network in which survivors can share their experiences and participate in discussions relating to sexual assault, in addition to having access to resources of help and support. Our website is completely anonymous to increase safety by preserving the identity of every user. In the future, we hope to expand this website to include a system where every college has stories, forums, and people of suspicion specific to that college.
